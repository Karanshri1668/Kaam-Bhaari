About

Kunal Pandagale, better known as Kaam Bhaari, is a Mumbai-based rapper, lyricist, and music producer. Rising from the streets of Kandivali, he broke out with his appearance in the Bollywood movie "Gully Boy" and later signed to Ranveer Singh's label IncInk Records. Known for his aggressive flow and raw lyrics, Kaam Bhaari represents the voice of the youth and the streets.
